Software EnerCa

EnerCa software is an advanced tool for the analysis and optimization of energy systems. It enables detailed assessment of energy consumption, evaluation of continuous measurement and statistical estimation of non-concurrent measurement. It also provides features to analyse energy payments and assess the efficiency of different energy sources, including photovoltaic and wind systems. EnerCa also supports the integration of energy storage and community sharing, using machine learning to suggest optimal allocations for maximum efficiency. The software emphasizes cost-effectiveness and optimal energy system configurations, which includes ROI analysis, risk identification and evaluation of energy sharing options.

What EnerCa offers

Energy Situation Analysis

The EnerCa software allows for the analysis of the current energy situation of buildings. The functionalities include:

  • Analysis of energy carrier consumption
  • Evaluation of continuous measurement data
  • Statistical estimation of non-continuous measurement data
  • Analysis of energy payment costs

Alternative Energy Sources and Storage

Evaluation of various energy sources, including photovoltaic and wind systems, can be performed. Energy storage can also be incorporated. Their combinations can be utilized. The software then evaluates production, self-consumption, and surpluses.

  • Photovoltaic systems
  • Wind systems
  • Cogeneration units
  • Battery systems

Community Sharing Optimization

Community sharing has seen a significant rise recently. The key question is how to properly set allocation keys to achieve the maximum sharing effect. EnerCa software can handle this as well, using machine learning algorithms to propose the optimal allocation.

  • Allocation optimization
  • Static, iterative, and dynamic allocation
  • Any periodicity for changing allocation keys (month, year)
  • Optimization of sharing groups
  • Sharing with and without the use of the distribution network

Economic Efficiency

Optimization based on economic efficiency is one of the key elements offered by the EnerCa software. By entering all parameters and various production and storage systems, the software seeks to find the optimal layout and configuration.

  • Optimization based on economic efficiency or maximum self-sufficiency
  • Evaluation from an investor's perspective
  • Paid and unpaid energy sharing
  • Risk identification (sensitivity analyses)
