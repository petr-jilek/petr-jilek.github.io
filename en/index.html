Software EnerCa

EnerCa software is an advanced tool for the analysis and optimization of energy systems. It enables detailed assessment of energy consumption, evaluation of continuous measurement and statistical estimation of non-concurrent measurement. It also provides features to analyse energy payments and assess the efficiency of different energy sources, including photovoltaic and wind systems. EnerCa also supports the integration of energy storage and community sharing, using machine learning to suggest optimal allocations for maximum efficiency. The software emphasizes cost-effectiveness and optimal energy system configurations, which includes ROI analysis, risk identification and evaluation of energy sharing options.
