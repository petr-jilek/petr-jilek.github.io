Petr Urban

Energy Project Specialist

Petr Urban is an electrical engineer and energy specialist with more than ten years of experience in the field. He has extensive experience in energy transformation, having worked at ČEPS a.s., where he was responsible for the development and maintenance of software controlling the entire transmission system in the Czech Republic. Later, he focused on electricity production, working as a management team member in a subsidiary of the Sev.en group. He was involved in the general repairs of several of the largest power plants in the Czech Republic.
